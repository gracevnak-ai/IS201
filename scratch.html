Genres I Love

Music hits different depending on the mood. Here's my breakdown:

  1. Pop
    • Catchy hooks and relatable lyrics
    • Artists like Taylor Swift, Olivia Rodrigo, Harry Styles
  2. R&B / Soul
    • Smooth production and emotional depth
    • Artists like SZA, Frank Ocean, Beyoncé
  3. Indie / Alternative
    • Unique sound and authentic storytelling
    • Artists like Phoebe Bridgers, Arctic Monkeys, Hozier
  4. Hip-Hop
    • Wordplay, rhythm, and cultural commentary
    • Artists like Kendrick Lamar, J. Cole, Drake
